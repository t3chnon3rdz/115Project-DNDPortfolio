Race: Goliath
Class: Barbarian
Subclass: Path of the Depths

Summary:

Very little is known about the mountain of a man Dimitri Orsinov. His more known adventure was with a small band of mercenaries who were tasked by an organization known as the "Dragon's Dogma", a group tasked in hunting down dragons causing trouble throughout the world. Dimitri tends to fight with a greatsword that rivals many, and fights with the strength of 10 men. Some have even said that Dimitri can teleport in order to take care of his enemies, though if that is the case, none have ever lived to see it. In his free time, Dimitri can be found drinking bars out of all their stock, and those that have dared compete in drinking games with him have described him as being the funniest half-wit they've ever met.

Fun Facts:

  • Dimitri's favourite drink is the Screwdriver (Vodka and orange juice).
