The D&D Archive of Keenan Clerx

Campaigns:

The Lost Lands Campaign

A campaign set on the lost continent of Alegritas. Filled with magic, mayhem, and mystery, there are many secrets for players to explore and unravel!

Light Of Xaryxis

A sci-fi adventure featuring a rag-tag set of heroes from outer space attempting to save their world being devoured by space elves.

After Hours

After Hours follows the tales of 6 emotionally-tired retail workers in a fantasy mall trying to survive all sorts of supernatural encursions!

[ARCHIVED] The Land of Aerenel

A campaign set within the land of Aerenel, our adventures look to stop the war between two ancient nations: Bulgost and Vilumia.

Characters:

Silas Sorrowgrave

A Rogue missing his heart (Literally, not figuratively) looking to re-acquire his humanity by following a string of tradgedies to find the answers he seeks.

Sybil Borden

A cleric of the Ceaseless Watcher, Sybil looks to find the hidden secrets behind his god and the disappearance of his family.

Dimitri Orsinov

Dimitri is a simple man from the Arctic Circle. Though he's not the brightest, he knows how to use his greatsword to solve his problems.

Galileo

Galileo is a small pygmy owlin who can see all potential futures in front of him. He's a little ditzy, but always means well!

Ezra North

One of the sons of the families of Cardinal Directions, Ezra is an archaeologist who is looking to revive his family after a terrible curse.
